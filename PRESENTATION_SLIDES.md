## Design Guidelines

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Clean, not cluttered
- Use real photos, not stock images
- Colors: Warm, earthy tones (saffron, green, white - Indian flag inspiration, but subtle)
- Font: Clear, large (Noto Sans or similar)
- Minimal text per slide (slides are visual aid, not script)

**What to Avoid:**
- Don't use corporate templates
- No flashy animations (distracting)
- No clip art (looks amateurish)
- No walls of text (death by PowerPoint)

**What Works:**
- One idea per slide
- Large, clear visuals
- Real photos from pilot (if available)
- Authentic, not polished to death

## Pro Tips

1. **Don't read slides** - They should support what you say, not repeat it
2. **Make eye contact** - With all judges, not just the one asking questions
3. **Use remote, not laptop** - Frees you to move and gesture
4. **Practice transitions** - Smooth flow between slides
5. **Have backup** - USB drive, email to yourself, cloud link
6. **Test beforehand** - Ensure slides render correctly on their system
7. **Know your slide count** - If you're at slide 10 and time is half over, you're on track
